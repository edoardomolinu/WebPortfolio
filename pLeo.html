Leo | Casa Bugatti

Small space, Grand Ritual

Following a meeting with Casa Bugatti, Leo was created: a toaster that transcends the role of a simple appliance to become a piece of art suited for the home environment.

With this concept in mind, the project emphasizes the bread-toasting experience through the lever, turning this action into a unique ritual.

Every component of Leo has been engineered and designed to translate its aesthetics and core concepts into a functional object ready for industrial-scale production.

Redefining the quick-meal ritual for the modern urbanite. The goal was to distill high-end culinary performance into a compact, intuitive experience, tailored for the sophisticated constraints of luxury micro-living.

A bold silhouette that reclaims the kitchen space. Leo merges Bugatti’s heritage with the modern micro-living ritual: high-end design without compromise.

Mechanism

Transforming a simple daily gesture into an iconic and unique ritual.

A bold handle interaction that redefines tradition through mechanical soul.

The Foot

Thermal Elevation. A slender pedestal grants Leo a weightless, sculptural presence.

Beyond its iconic silhouette, this architectural feature is engineered for high-performance heat dissipation, optimizing airflow through a refined and airy structure.

Control Knob

A singular dial for total command. Navigate through 7 precise browning levels with a smooth, calibrated rotation.

The central STOP button is flanked by illuminated modes for Defrost, Reheat, and a dedicated Exterior Coil setting for perfect results.
