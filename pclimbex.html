Climbex | Politecnico di Milano

Biomimicry is the practice of studying nature's designs, processes, and strategies to solve human problems. It involves observing how organisms have adapted over time and applying those insights to innovate and improve our own technologies and systems.

The theme of our exploration lies hidden among the mountain ranges. It is here that we rediscover a long-lost connection to nature, attempting to close the gap by observing and mimicking the behaviors of the wildlife that calls it home.

Our project is directed to the closely knit community of climbers and outdoor enthusiasts seeking:

  1. Performance through bio-inspired innovation
  2. Deep habitat reconnection

More than a sport, it reflects a lifestyle and a way to interact and be part of nature.
