LuminAir | Midea Group

Beyond Wash: Active Protection

The project originated from a collaboration with Midea Group, driven by the goal of finding an innovative solution to enhance their dishwasher cavity.

By exploring market needs and emerging trends within the company's sector, LuminAir was born: a module that utilizes a catalytic reaction to eliminate odors and sanitize the dishwasher interior between washing cycles.

Elevating the high-capacity dishwashing experience through active environment management. The goal was to solve air stagnation and bacterial proliferation during extended loading, merging photocatalytic purification with intelligent hydration for a consistently sanitized, odor-free cavity.
